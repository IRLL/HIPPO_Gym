Practice makes perfect

  • You can get better at planning through practice.
  • You will begin with a pre-test of 2 game rounds. This will give us (the researchers) a better understanding on your starting game performance.
  • This will be followed by 20 practice rounds. During these practice trials, an intelligent tutor will give you helpful feedback on how to decide what to do.
  • After the practice trials, there will be a test block of 10 rounds. During this test block, the intelliegent tutor will not be available. The goal is to use what you have learned during the practice rounds to help improve your test score.
  • The higher your score is in the test block, the higher your bonus will be.
