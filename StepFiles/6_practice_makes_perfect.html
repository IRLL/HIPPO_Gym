Practice makes perfect

  • You can get better at planning through practice.
  • There will be 3 blocks of the study (1) Pre-test block (2) Training block and (3) Test block.
  • The pre-test block will consist of 2 trials. During these trials you will be playing Web of Cash. The purpose of these trials is to determine your baseline score.
  • The training block will consist of 20 trials. During these trials, you will practice playing Web of Cash.
  • Lastly, there will be the test block of 10 trials where you can use what you have learned to earn a bonus.
  • The better you perform, the higher your bonsus will be.
  • An intelligent tutor will give you helpful feedback on how you decide what to do.
