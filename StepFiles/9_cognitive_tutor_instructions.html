Intelligent Tutor

The intelligent tutor will give you helpful feedback on how to decide what to do in the Flight Planning game. The tutor's feedback can come in different forms

  • The tutor can provide feedback on the quality of your planning decisions.
  • The tutor can explain why your decisions were good/bad. If this feedback is available, there will be a question mark symbol. You must click on the symbol to see the entire explanation.
  • The tutor can provide suggestions on how to improve your decision-making strategies in the game.
