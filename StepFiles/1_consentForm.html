Invitation to Participate:

We invite you to participate in this study about analyzing how people make decisions and investigating strategies to improve human decision-making.

Purpose of the Study:

This study aims to show that we can teach humans how to make better long-term decisions and avoid acting myopically. By acting myopically better decisions, we mean making decisions that help only in the short term how to act in a given situation so that the long-term advantage of taking such an action is maximized.

Participation:

: If you wish to participate in this study, you will be asked to first complete a short survey on your decision-making strategies. Next, you will complete a second task in which you will be interacting with a game. During the game, you will be provided feedback on how to improve your decision-making in the game. This is followed by another short survey. The study should take you approximately 30 minutes to complete. You do not have to answer any questions that you are not comfortable with. However, you must answer all attention check questions. You may stop at any point by closing the window and no data further data will be collected. Any data collected from previous components will be deleted. You will still receive the default compensation for completing the previous sub-tasks successfully. However, to receive the entire compensation of $7.50, you must complete all three tasks together (i.e., no stopping between tasks).

Benefits:

There are no direct benefits of taking part in this study except for (hopefully) enjoying these different tasks.

Risks:

This study involves minimal risk which includes minor fatigue due to the concentration required to finish the task. There is also the normal risk of interacting with a computer, e.g., long-term symptoms from muscle reuse like carpel tunnel syndromes. You can withdraw from the study at any point till submitting the response.

Confidentiality and Anonymity:

All results from the study will remain confidential. We will only use results from the study for the purposes of this research. The only people who will have access to the research data are the Principal Investigators and researchers working on this study. Results will be published in pooled (aggregate) format. Your data will be anonymous as you will not provide your name or any personal information.

Data Storage:

The results will come from the surveys and logs based on your game performance. This data will be stored in AWS S3 within their Canadian data centers. It will be stored for at least 5 years following the completion of the study. Only researchers on this project will get access to this data. If you would like to withdraw your data from the study, please email us using the email address provided. To complete this removal request, you must send us an email within 30 days of completing the study. In such email, you must include the UUID you received during the study on the instructions and final page. If you request removal of your data, you will still retain any incentive earned.

Compensation (or Reimbursement):

Participants will be paid a compensation of $7.50 for completing the entire study. To receive the full $7.50 payment, you must complete all 3 components of the study. However, you will receive partial payment for completing individual components of the study as outlined in detail below. Also, you must answer all attention check questions correctly. You will be prompted by the system if you fail any of the attention check questions. If this occurs, you will get one more opportunity to answer the attention check questions correctly. If you fail to do so, you will be forced to quit the study at that point, but you will still get any incentive previously earned. This is outlined in detail under Task 1 below. Our expectation is that anyone who puts forth a good effort (e.g., not multi-tasking or watching television) will easily pass such checks. Also, participants can receive a performance-based bonus of $1.50. Participants will receive a bonus of $0.01 for every in-game score of 5. For example, if the participant gets a score of 500, their bonus will be (500/5)*0.01 = $1.00.

We have task-specific compensation for completing each task as outlined below:

Task 1: If you complete the first task, you will be paid $0.50 as compensation. This task should take 5 min on average to complete. This task involves answering survey questions and reading through the game instructions. You will have to answer the attention check questions correctly to go to the next task. You will have two attempts to get 100% on all attention check questions. If you fail to answer all attention check questions correctly, then the study will end, and you will only receive $0.50 as compensation.

Task 2: In the second task, you will be interacting with a game. You will be receiving feedback to improve your score on the game. There will be 30 rounds in total. This task should take 15 minutes on average to complete. If you complete the second task, you will receive $6.50. During this phase, you will receive a bonus of $0.01 for every in-game score of 5. For example, if you get a score of 500, then your bonus will be (500/5)*0.01 = $1.00. The maximum bonus is $1.50. You will be told your bonus amount at the completion of this task.

Task 3: In the final task, you will answer another round of survey questions. This task should take approximately 5 min to complete. If you complete this last task, you will receive $0.50.

As a reminder, you cannot proceed to the next task without completing the previous task. So, if you only complete Task 1 and then withdraw from the study, you will only receive the $0.50 payment. The same goes for Task 2. If you only complete Task 1 and Task 2 and not the final Task 3 and then withdraw from the study, you will only receive the combined $7.00 payment and any bonus amount you received from Task 2

Voluntary Participation:

You are under no obligation to take part and if you choose to, you may refuse to answer questions that you do not want to answer, except for the attention check questions. Should you choose to withdraw midway through the electronic survey simply close the link and no further responses will be included. We will also delete any data collected from previous components. You will still receive the default compensation for completing the previous sub-tasks successfully. However, to receive the entire compensation of $7.50, you must complete all three tasks together (i.e., no stopping between tasks).
